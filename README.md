# Digital Marketing Internship Project – Quran Tutoring Business

## Project Status

Ongoing (Week 1–3 Completed out of 6 Weeks)

## Project Overview

This project is being completed as part of a 6-week Digital Marketing Internship. The objective is to apply practical digital marketing skills to a real Quran tutoring business, "Nayyab Eman Quran Tutor", by improving its online presence, audience engagement, and marketing performance.

## Completed Work (Week 1–3)

### Week 1 – Research & Campaign Planning

* Business Introduction
* Market Research
* Target Audience Analysis
* Competitor Analysis
* SMART Goals & KPIs
* Content Strategy
* Content Calendar

### Week 2 – Content Creation & Campaign Execution

* 4 Social Media Posts
* 3 Short-form Reels
* Sample Email Campaign
* 2 Mock Advertisement Campaigns
* Audience Engagement Monitoring
* Comments & DM Tracking

### Week 3 – Performance Analysis & Optimization

* Facebook Insights Analysis
* Instagram Insights Analysis
* Reach & Engagement Tracking
* Content Performance Evaluation
* Optimization Analysis
* A/B Testing
* Weekly Reflection
